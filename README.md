## 💫 About Me

I'm a **Senior Software Engineer & Technical Leader** based in **Abuja, Nigeria**, with 8+ years of experience building scalable, high-impact digital platforms across **digital health**, **fintech**, **government systems**, and **consumer applications**.

I specialize in full-stack and mobile development — React, React Native, Node.js, Django — and I'm passionate about using technology to solve real problems for African users.

Currently, I serve as **Senior Coordinator of Software Engineering at [eHealth Africa](https://www.eHealthAfrica.org)**, where I lead development of platforms that have **digitized millions of immunization records** and improved supply chain visibility across Nigerian health facilities.

As **Founder & CEO of [NanleTech](https://nanletech.com)**, I lead an agency delivering high-impact digital solutions including:
- 🌾 **NiFMIS** — Nigeria's national Farmer Management Information System, built in partnership with the **National Bureau of Statistics** and the **Tony Blair Institute for Global Change**
- 💰 **Finanza** — an intuitive expense management and budgeting platform
- ✨ **Tabspire** — a Chrome extension that transforms new tabs into moments of inspiration

> 🏆 **NASA SpaceApps Challenge** — 1st Place (State Level) · 3rd Place (National Level)

## 🤖 Latest: AI Integration

I recently integrated an **LLM-powered chatbot into LoMIS**, enabling field health workers to query real-time logistics and supply chain data using natural language. This involved prompt architecture design, backend API integration, and streaming response implementation for low-latency UX in field environments.

I also use AI-assisted design tools (**Stitch**, **Lovable**) to rapidly prototype UIs from user stories — bridging the gap between product requirements and development without a dedicated designer.
